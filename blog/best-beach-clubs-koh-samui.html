Best Beach Clubs in Koh Samui

Sunbeds, cocktails, DJs and ocean views — the complete guide to Koh Samui's beach club scene.

Home › Beach Clubs › Best Beach Clubs in Koh Samui

Koh Samui is one of Thailand’s best islands for beach clubs. Over the past decade the island has developed a lively beach club scene ranging from relaxed sunset bars to international party venues with DJs, pools and daybeds.

Some beach clubs focus on chilled afternoons with cocktails and ocean views, while others turn into lively sunset parties. In this guide we share the best beach clubs in Koh Samui, what atmosphere to expect, and when you should go.

Top Beach Clubs in Koh Samui

Nikki Beach — Lipa Noi

Nikki Beach is the most famous beach club on Koh Samui and part of the international Nikki Beach brand. Located on the west coast of the island in Lipa Noi, the venue is known for its stylish white design, pool parties and legendary Sunday brunch events.

Expect a lively atmosphere with DJs, cocktails and large groups celebrating birthdays or special occasions. Minimum spend usually ranges between 2,000 and 3,000 THB on weekends.

Ark Bar — Chaweng Beach

Ark Bar is the busiest beach club in Chaweng and a classic Koh Samui party spot. Located directly on the main stretch of Chaweng Beach, it attracts a mix of backpackers, travellers and groups looking for a fun beach atmosphere.

Pool parties start early afternoon and the energy builds throughout the day. There is usually no minimum spend which makes it a good option for travellers looking for a lively but affordable beach club.

Beach Republic — Lamai

Beach Republic is one of the most stylish beach clubs in Koh Samui and located in Lamai. The venue combines a beach club, restaurant and boutique hotel with a relaxed but sophisticated atmosphere.

It attracts a slightly older crowd than Chaweng venues and is popular for sunset cocktails and relaxed afternoon sessions by the pool.

CocoTam's — Bophut

CocoTam's in Fisherman's Village is technically more of a beach bar than a traditional beach club, but it is one of the most loved sunset spots on the island.

The vibe is relaxed with beanbags on the sand, cocktails, fire shows in the evening and a friendly mix of locals and visitors.

Jungle Club — Chaweng Hills

Jungle Club sits high above Chaweng Bay and offers one of the best views on Koh Samui. Although not technically a beach club, it deserves a place on this list thanks to its panoramic location and laid-back vibe.

Come here for sunset drinks, incredible views and a more relaxed bohemian atmosphere compared to the beach level clubs.

What to Expect at Koh Samui Beach Clubs

Prices and Minimum Spend

Many beach clubs operate on a minimum spend system rather than a fixed entrance fee. Depending on the venue this can range from 500 to 3,000 THB per person.

Best Time to Go

The best time to visit most beach clubs is between midday and sunset. The atmosphere usually builds around 2–3pm when DJs start playing and the crowd grows.

Beach Clubs by Area

Chaweng: the busiest nightlife and beach club area on the island.

Lamai: slightly quieter and more relaxed beach clubs.

Bophut: sunset bars and beach lounges near Fisherman's Village.

Lipa Noi: home of Nikki Beach and famous sunset views.

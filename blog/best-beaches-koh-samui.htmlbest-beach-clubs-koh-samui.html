Best Beaches in Koh Samui

From lively Chaweng to quiet tropical bays and sunset-perfect west coast sands — this is our honest guide to the best beaches in Koh Samui.

Home › Beaches › Best Beaches in Koh Samui
Beaches Updated March 2026 7 min read

Koh Samui has no shortage of beaches, but not all of them offer the same experience. Some are made for long lazy days with restaurants and beach bars within walking distance, while others are best if you want calm water, fewer people and a more relaxed atmosphere.

This guide covers the best beaches in Koh Samui depending on what kind of day you want: swimming, sunsets, families, quiet beach walks or a livelier stretch close to restaurants and nightlife.

🌴

Quick takeaway: Chaweng is the most famous and lively, Lamai is the best all-rounder, Maenam is ideal for peace and quiet, Silver Beach is the prettiest small bay, and Lipa Noi is the sunset favourite.

Best Beaches in Koh Samui

01
Best Overall

Chaweng Beach

Chaweng Beach is Koh Samui’s best-known beach and for many visitors it is the obvious first stop. The sand is soft, the sea is usually clear and calm, and there is always something happening nearby. Hotels, beach clubs, massage spots, cafés and restaurants all sit within easy reach.

This is the beach to choose if you want energy, convenience and a classic tropical Koh Samui feel. The trade-off is that it can get busy, especially in high season and around the more central areas.

02
Best All-Rounder

Lamai Beach

Lamai Beach is often the sweet spot for travellers who want a beautiful beach without the full-on Chaweng energy. It still has plenty of restaurants, bars and places to stay, but the overall atmosphere feels calmer and a little more relaxed.

If you want a beach that works for both swimming and evenings out, Lamai is a very strong choice. It feels livelier than Maenam but less hectic than Chaweng.

03
Quiet Escape

Maenam Beach

On the north coast, Maenam Beach offers a quieter and more laid-back side of Koh Samui. You get long stretches of sand, swaying palms and a view across to Koh Phangan. It is the sort of beach where you can actually hear the water and not much else.

This is one of the best beaches on the island if you want a slower pace, peaceful mornings and fewer crowds. It suits couples, families and longer-stay travellers especially well.

04
Most Beautiful Bay

Silver Beach

Silver Beach is smaller than the others on this list, but visually it is one of the loveliest beaches on Koh Samui. The bay feels tucked away, the water is often bright and clear, and it has that postcard look many people imagine when they think of Thailand.

Because it is smaller, it can feel busier than the longer beaches, but it is still absolutely worth visiting for a swim, a relaxed afternoon or just the scenery alone.

05
Best Sunset

Lipa Noi Beach

Lipa Noi sits on the west coast of the island and is best known for its sunsets. The beach is wide, peaceful and much less crowded than the east coast beaches. By late afternoon the light here turns golden and the whole beach feels softer and slower.

It is also one of the best beaches for families because the water is generally shallow and calm. If your ideal beach day ends with a sunset drink rather than a party, this is the place.

Which Beach in Koh Samui is Best for You?

Best for nightlife and restaurants: Chaweng Beach

Best all-round option: Lamai Beach

Best for peace and quiet: Maenam Beach

Best for a small tropical bay: Silver Beach

Best for sunsets and families: Lipa Noi Beach

Practical Tips Before You Go

Best beach for first-time visitors

If it is your first time in Koh Samui, Chaweng or Lamai usually make the most sense. Both give you beach, restaurants and easy access to other parts of the island.

Best beach for quiet mornings

Maenam and Lipa Noi are much calmer than the main east coast beaches. They are especially good if you want a slower pace.

Best beach for sunset

Lipa Noi wins here. Because it faces west, it gets the kind of sunset light that east coast beaches simply do not.

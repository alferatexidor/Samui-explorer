Best Beaches in Koh Samui

From lively party beaches to quiet tropical bays — these are the best beaches in Koh Samui.

Home › Beaches › Best Beaches in Koh Samui

Koh Samui is famous for its tropical beaches, crystal-clear water and relaxed island atmosphere. The island offers a wide variety of beaches — from lively stretches filled with beach bars and restaurants to quiet hidden bays where you can enjoy the ocean almost entirely to yourself.

In this guide we share the best beaches in Koh Samui, including where to swim, where to watch the sunset and which beaches are best if you want peace and quiet.

Best Beaches in Koh Samui

Chaweng Beach

Chaweng Beach is the most famous beach on Koh Samui and the centre of the island’s nightlife. The beach stretches for several kilometres with soft white sand and clear turquoise water.

During the day it’s perfect for swimming and beach walks, while at night the nearby bars and restaurants create a lively atmosphere.

Lamai Beach

Lamai Beach is often considered the perfect balance between relaxation and activity. It’s slightly quieter than Chaweng but still offers plenty of restaurants, beach bars and places to stay.

The water is usually calm and great for swimming, and the sunsets here are particularly beautiful.

Maenam Beach

Maenam Beach on the north coast of Koh Samui is known for its peaceful atmosphere and stunning views of Koh Phangan. The beach is lined with palm trees and offers a much quieter experience compared to the busier east coast beaches.

It’s a great place to relax, take long walks along the sand and enjoy a slower pace of island life.

Silver Beach

Silver Beach is a small but incredibly beautiful bay located between Chaweng and Lamai. Many travellers consider it one of the most picturesque beaches on the island.

The water here is often very clear, making it a great spot for swimming and snorkelling.

Lipa Noi Beach

Lipa Noi Beach is located on the west coast of Koh Samui and is known for its incredible sunsets. The beach is wide, peaceful and rarely crowded.

It’s also one of the best beaches for families because the water is shallow and calm.

Which Beach Should You Choose?

If you want nightlife and restaurants, Chaweng Beach is the best choice. If you prefer something slightly quieter but still lively, Lamai Beach is a great option.

For peace and quiet, Maenam Beach is ideal, while Silver Beach offers one of the most beautiful small bays on the island.
